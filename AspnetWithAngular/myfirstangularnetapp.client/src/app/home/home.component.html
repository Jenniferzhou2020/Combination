GDCS (Generic Data Collection System)

GDCS is a powerful web application designed to streamline data collection for business users. It enables efficient gathering of data from both internal and external users, simplifying the review and analysis process.

Key Features:

  • COA (Chapter of Accounts) Management – Organize and manage financial accounts with ease.
  • Template Management – Design, approve, and publish templates using online/offline tools.
  • Submission Management – Facilitate seamless data submission and approval workflows.
  • Reporting – Generate insights with comprehensive reporting capabilities.
  • User & Role Management – Control access and permissions for secure collaboration.

GDCS empowers businesses to collect, manage, and analyze data efficiently, saving time and improving decision-making.
